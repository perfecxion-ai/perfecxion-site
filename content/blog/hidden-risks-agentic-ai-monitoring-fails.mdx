### The Baseline Problem
Security teams love baselines. Normal behavior patterns that help identify anomalies. But agentic AI systems don't have baselinesthey have objectives. Their behavior changes based on:
- Current goals and sub-goals
- Environmental conditions
- Learning from previous actions
- Interactions with other agents
- Resource availability
- Success/failure feedback loops

### Risk 3: Emergent Behavior Cascades
The most dangerous risks arise from emergent behaviorspatterns that weren't programmed but develop through the interaction of simple rules at scale. These behaviors are impossible to predict and often invisible until they reach critical mass.
