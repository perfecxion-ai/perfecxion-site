### Security Standards and Frameworks

Beyond data protection, AI systems must meet established security standards that often predate AI but still apply:

- **SOC 2 Type II**: Service organization controls for security, availability, and confidentiality
- **ISO 27001**: International standard for information security management
- **NIST Cybersecurity Framework**: Voluntary framework becoming mandatory in many sectors
- **PCI DSS**: Required for any AI system processing payment card data
- **HIPAA**: Healthcare-specific requirements for AI processing patient data
